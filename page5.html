Texas Hold'em Poker Game
started January 2026 ---- completion TBD
  • For fun, I created an online poker card game of Texas Hold'em.
  • The intent is to create a similar poker game as seen on Clup WPT and other online poker sites.
  • The difference will be to set up scenarios of Bad Beat hands and challenge the user to beat the bad beat if possible.
  • If they don't choose correctly, they learn a valuable lesson. It's a training model. Better to learn from your mistakes here than at a live poker table.
